About Us

IEM-UTM Student Section (IEM-UTMSS) is a student-run organization that represents the Institution of Engineers, Malaysia (IEM) at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM). Our mission is to promote the engineering profession and provide a platform for students to develop their professional and soft skills.

Objective

  • To guide Year 1 to Year 4 engineering students in actively planning their career paths, equipping them with the essential skills and readiness needed for the professional workforce.
  • To provide participants with actionable lessons, insider knowledge, and practical mentorship from professionals, ensuring they have the guidance required to succeed in the engineering industry.
  • To assist students in identifying and securing suitable job opportunities that perfectly align with their specific engineering disciplines, technical strengths, and professional interests.
  • To facilitate meaningful networking between students and industry leaders, building strong professional relationships that open doors for local employment and overseas career advancements.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who can join the event?
Day 3 of Employability Series 5.0 is exclusively open to engineering students from Year 1 to Year 4.
2. How many companies are participating?
A total of 4 companies will join us. These companies come from a variety of engineering sectors, including top mechanical and semiconductor firms. Our participating partners include Solarvest, Mascot Engineering & Construction, Bridge Data Centres, and Juken Technology Engineering Sdn. Bhd.
3. Can I interview with more than one company?
Participants are assigned randomly and will only have one interview slot.
4. When will the participant list be released?
The list will be published once the total number of participants is finalized.
5. Who should I inform if I can't attend?
Contact the organizers:
SOH ENG KEONG | +60162080208
EMILY FAN CHONG XIAN | +60136313266
6. Is food provided?
Yes, bread and water is provided during the event.
7. What should I prepare?
Participants are required to wear formal clothing and bring their resume to the event.
8. Can I leave after my interview?
Yes, participants are allowed to leave after completing their mock interview session. However, we highly encourage everyone to gather back at 5:20 PM and stay for the closing ceremony.
9. How long is each interview?
Each participant will be allocated approximately 35 minutes for their mock interview session, with an additional 5-minute buffer time for feedback or concluding remarks from the interviewer.
